FoxPRO-HR Complete Software Specification

1. Purpose

FoxPRO-HR is a centralized web-based HR platform that automates employee records, attendance, leave, payroll, recruitment, performance, training, documentation, scheduling, analytics, role-based access, notifications, and system configuration to reduce manual work and improve data accuracy in real-time.

2. Core Modules

  1. Employee Management
  2. Attendance Management
  3. Leave Management
  4. Payroll Management
  5. Recruitment Management
  6. Performance Management
  7. Training & Development
  8. Document Management
  9. Shift & Scheduling
  10. Reporting & Analytics
  11. User Roles & Permission Management
  12. Notification & Communication
  13. System Settings & Configuration

3. Functional Requirements by Module

Employee Management: profile creation, CNIC, address, phone, email, department, designation, joining date, salary, emergency contact, document attachment, status lifecycle.

Attendance: check-in/out, biometric/device input hooks, GPS/device fingerprint, late detection, overtime and reporting.

Leave: annual/sick/casual leaves, balances, request workflow, manager approval and history.

Payroll: salary structures, allowances, deductions, overtime, tax and monthly payslip generation.

Recruitment: job posting, candidate applications, resumes, interview schedules, evaluation and hiring decision.

Performance: KPI tracking, manager reviews, self-assessment and performance reporting.

Training: schedules, courses, enrollments and certification tracking.

Documents: contracts, certificates, policy files, expiry tracking.

Scheduling: shifts, assignments, roster calendar and compliance support.

Reporting: dashboard KPIs, export-ready reports, filter/search and drill-downs.

RBAC: Super Admin, HR Manager, Manager, Employee roles with permissions.

Notifications: leave alerts, payroll ready alerts, announcements via in-app notifications.

System Settings: organization profile, attendance policy, leave policy, tax slabs, payroll cycle and audit logs.

4. Database Entities (Implemented)

Employee, Department, Designation, Attendance, Leave_Request, Payroll, Salary_Structure, Recruitment, Candidate, Performance_Review, Training, User, Role, Permission, Notification plus helper entities for document and shift assignments.

5. UI/UX Requirements

Responsive mobile-first layout, role-based menus, modern cards, search/filter architecture, transition animations, fast loading screens, and real-time ready notification area.

6. Professional Additions (Recommended)

  • Audit trail for sensitive actions
  • 2FA and password policy enforcement
  • Data backup/restore policy
  • API layer for biometric devices
  • Email/SMS gateway integration
